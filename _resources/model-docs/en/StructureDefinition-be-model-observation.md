## Additional Information

This observation model is used to capture clinical observations and measurements in the Belgian healthcare context.

### Usage Guidelines

- Use this model for recording vital signs, laboratory results, and other clinical measurements
- Always include appropriate LOINC or SNOMED codes
- Ensure proper referencing to the patient and encounter

### Examples

Common use cases include:
- Blood pressure measurements
- Body temperature
- Laboratory test results
- Physical examination findings

For more information, see the [FHIR Observation resource](http://hl7.org/fhir/observation.html).
